What's the purpose of this site?

I create a lot of notes and I like markdown. I searched for days for a note taking system that's open source, supports LaTeX and most importantly, is easy to use. Turns out that my mistake was not looking at Julia as an option. I was reading Julia's docs and the Documenter.jl caught my eye. I looked into it and I was impressed. So, I deleted the old website (the distill one) and here we are. There is a learning curve to this but, whatever.
